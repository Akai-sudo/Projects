Ever since I was a child, I have been interested in everything related to art. I remember how it felt when I painted my first painting ... the feeling completely overwhelmed me. As is the case with many individuals, my mum played an important role in supporting my passion for art. For that, I’ll be grateful to her and will aim to pass this on to other people.
As I got older I found myself in graphic design and marketing. I still remember how very proud I was of myself when I made my first flyer.When I first got to know the apps for the production of graphic design, my habits changed completely.
I started doing everything in that direction to become successful. All my time has been consumed by creating. And creating is my job, so I never feel like I’m actually working. I’ve always said to myself that I want a job from which I don’t need a vacation. And that's exactly what I found.I am a very positive person and I see an opportunity to learn in all my mistakes. I love what I do and I am very committed to it. In my free time, I like to read science fiction books, go to exhibitions or sometimes paint.I am still studying at the moment, working at Agado Agency and freelancing on the side.Specialities: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Marketing, Infographics, Typography, Graphic Design.
